## Local Business Features — South Florida

Every week, Moments by LP photographs and features a local business in Boca Raton and South Florida.

From the coffee shop where Lauren edits every morning to the boutique hotel that makes visitors fall in love with Palm Beach County — these are the businesses worth knowing, told through the lens of a local photographer.

### Why We Feature Local Businesses

South Florida's small businesses are the heartbeat of the community. Every feature includes professional photography and an editorial write-up that businesses can share, that Google can index, and that the community can discover.
