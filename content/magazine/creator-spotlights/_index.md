## Creator Spotlights — South Florida

South Florida is home to an incredible community of photographers, designers, coaches, entrepreneurs, artists, and makers.

This is where their stories live.

Each Creator Spotlight is an in-depth feature — photographed by Lauren and written to capture not just what they do, but *why* they do it. The kind of story that attracts clients, builds community, and sticks in people's minds long after they've read it.

### Who Gets Spotlighted

- Photographers and videographers
- Graphic designers and brand strategists
- Coaches (business, life, fitness, wellness)
- Entrepreneurs and founders
- Artists, muralists, and makers
- Real estate professionals
- Stylists, makeup artists, and hair artists
