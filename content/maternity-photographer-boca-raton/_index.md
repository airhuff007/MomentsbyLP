## Maternity Photography in Boca Raton, FL

This season of life is fleeting. A maternity session is how you hold onto it.

Lauren creates soft, natural, and genuinely beautiful maternity portraits for expecting mothers throughout Boca Raton and South Florida — at the beach, in the golden hour light, or in the comfort of your home.

## Maternity Session Styles

**Beach Maternity Sessions**
South Florida's beaches at golden hour are a stunning backdrop for maternity portraits. Warm light, soft waves, flowing fabric — breathtaking every time.

**Outdoor & Garden Sessions**
Boca Raton's parks, botanical gardens, and outdoor locations offer lush, natural settings for maternity portraits that feel timeless.

**In-Home Lifestyle Sessions**
Documentary-style maternity photography in your own home — capturing the real, everyday beauty of pregnancy. The nursery, your partner, your other children.

**Studio-Style Sessions**
Clean backgrounds, soft window light, and an intimate atmosphere that keeps the focus entirely on you.

## When to Schedule Your Maternity Session

The ideal time for maternity photos is **28–34 weeks** — your bump is beautifully round, and you're still comfortable and mobile. Lauren recommends booking early, especially for beach sessions at golden hour during South Florida's peak season (October–April).

## What to Wear for Maternity Photos

Lauren provides a full styling guide with your booking confirmation. In general: flowy dresses photograph beautifully, especially at the beach. Neutral and earthy tones work well in South Florida's natural light. Bring your partner and older children — the more the merrier.
