## Frequently Asked Questions

**When should we do our engagement session in Boca Raton?**
As soon as possible after getting engaged — ideally 4–8 months before your wedding. This gives you time to use your photos for save-the-dates and gives you both practice being photographed together.

**What should couples wear for photos in South Florida?**
Coordinate, don't match. Think about the location — flowy dresses and linen shirts photograph beautifully at the beach. Avoid busy patterns or logos. Bring a second outfit for variety.
