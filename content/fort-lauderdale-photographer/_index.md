- **Las Olas Boulevard** — Fort Lauderdale&rsquo;s most iconic street. Restaurant terraces, boutiques, and beautiful golden-hour light

- **The Riverwalk** — waterfront promenade with the New River and the Fort Lauderdale skyline
- **Flagler Village** — arts district with murals, industrial character, and creative energy
- **Bonnet House Museum &amp; Gardens** — lush, historic, and deeply photogenic
