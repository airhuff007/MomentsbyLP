## South Florida Creator Community

You don&rsquo;t have to build your creative career alone.

The Moments by LP Creator Community is a growing network of photographers, entrepreneurs, coaches, stylists, designers, and creatives in South Florida. Together, we collaborate, create, and grow.

## What the Community Offers

### Portfolio Days
Collaborative shooting days in curated locations throughout Boca Raton and South Florida. Work with models, styled sets, and creative teams — all for the purpose of building your portfolio and sharpening your eye.

### Styled Shoots
Intentional, editorial-style shoots designed around themes, aesthetics, and locations. Perfect for photographers building a portfolio, brands creating campaign content, and creatives who want to experiment.

### Casting Calls
Open calls for models, subjects, and collaborators for upcoming Moments by LP projects. Published and promoted to the full community.

### Creative Events
Workshops, meetups, portfolio reviews, and creative gatherings in Boca Raton and throughout South Florida.

### Member Spotlights
Every month, a community member is spotlighted in the Moments by LP Magazine &mdash; a free editorial feature that gets them seen.

### Creator Resources
Templates, guides, and educational content for photographers and creative entrepreneurs in South Florida.

## Who Should Join

- Photographers (all levels) in South Florida
- Models and talent in Palm Beach County
- Creative entrepreneurs and brand builders
- Stylists, makeup artists, and hair artists
- Coaches and wellness professionals building a personal brand
