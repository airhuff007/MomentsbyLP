## Lifestyle Photography in Boca Raton &amp; South Florida

Not every great photo is posed. Sometimes the best images happen in between — when you forget the camera is there.

Lifestyle photography is about capturing real life beautifully. Lauren approaches every session with a documentary eye, creating images that feel effortless and authentic rather than staged.

## What Is Lifestyle Photography?

Lifestyle photography sits between traditional portrait photography (posed, studio-style) and documentary photography (purely candid). Lauren directs you naturally — giving you activities, prompts, and gentle guidance — while staying out of the way enough to capture real expressions and genuine moments.

The result: photos that look like the best version of your everyday life.
