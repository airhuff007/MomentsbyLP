**What should we wear for family photos in Boca Raton?**
Coordinate without matching. Stick to a palette of 2–3 colors that complement each other. For beach sessions in South Florida, light, flowy fabrics photograph beautifully.

Golden hour — the hour before sunset — gives the warmest, most flattering light for outdoor sessions in Boca Raton and along the South Florida coast. October through April is ideal for outdoor sessions.
