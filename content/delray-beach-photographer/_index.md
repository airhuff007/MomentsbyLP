## Delray Beach Photographer

Just south of Boca Raton, **Delray Beach** has become one of the most vibrant and photogenic cities in all of South Florida — and one of Lauren&rsquo;s favorite places to shoot.

## Why Delray Beach for Photos?

Delray Beach offers a completely different photographic energy from Boca Raton:

- **Atlantic Avenue** — the walkable, lively main street with boutiques, restaurants, and beautiful architecture that photographs brilliantly at golden hour
- **Delray Beach Oceanfront** — a wider, more open beach than many stretches of the Boca coast
- **Pineapple Grove** — arts district with murals, color, and creative urban energy
- **The Intracoastal** — waterfront settings with boats, docks, and reflective water
- **Wakodahatchee Wetlands** — one-of-a-kind boardwalk experience through native Florida wetlands
