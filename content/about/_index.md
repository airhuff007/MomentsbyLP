## About Lauren

Hi. I&rsquo;m Lauren.

I&rsquo;m a photographer based in Boca Raton, Florida, and I&rsquo;ve been shooting portraits and lifestyle photography in South Florida for years. But honestly — I&rsquo;m more than a photographer. I&rsquo;m a storyteller.

## Why Photography

I didn&rsquo;t pick up a camera because I wanted a career. I picked one up because I noticed that the moments that matter most have a way of slipping past unnoticed. The laugh that happens between the posed shots. The way a couple looks at each other when they think no one&rsquo;s watching. The chaos of a family morning that feels exhausting in the moment and priceless a decade later.

Photography is how I slow that down.

## What I Believe

I believe that the best photos aren&rsquo;t taken. They&rsquo;re created — through trust, through ease, through making people forget there&rsquo;s a camera in the room.

I believe that every family, every brand, and every business in South Florida has a story worth telling well. Not a generic story. *Their* story.

And I believe that images should make you feel something — not just look good on a wall.

## Life in Boca Raton

I live in Boca Raton and I love it here. The light is different in South Florida than anywhere else I&rsquo;ve ever been — that golden hour on the beach is something you have to experience to understand.

When I&rsquo;m not shooting, you&rsquo;ll find me at one of Boca&rsquo;s coffee shops, exploring a new restaurant in Delray Beach, or walking the beach at Spanish River Park.

Some of my favorite places in South Florida:

- **Coffee:** My go-to is a local Boca Raton spot with great natural light (perfect for brand sessions too)
- **Dinner:** The Delray Beach restaurant scene is endlessly great
- **Photo scouting:** Spanish River Park at golden hour, every time
- **Creative recharge:** Morikami Museum — the Japanese gardens are stunning

## The Moments by LP Mission

Moments by LP isn&rsquo;t just a photography studio. It&rsquo;s a platform for South Florida stories.

Through the [Moments by LP Magazine](/magazine/), I feature local businesses, creators, and places that deserve to be known. Through the [Creator Community](/creator-community/), I support the photographers and creatives building their craft in South Florida.

Photography is the entry point. Community is the mission.
